Test Connectivity: You should be able to ping between computers in the ACCOUNTS and DELIVERY departments now. For example, from a computer in the ACCOUNTS department, you should be able to ping computers in the DELIVERY department and vice versa. This setup segregates the two departments into different VLANs, allowing them to communicate through the router. The router serves as a gateway between the two VLANs, allowing traffic to flow between them while keeping them logically separate.
